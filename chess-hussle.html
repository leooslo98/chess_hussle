HOW IT WORKS
01

Connect Phantom or Solflare

Click Connect Wallet and approve the connection in your browser. We never see your private key.

02

Post a Game & Lock SOL

Set your wager. You sign a Solana transaction that locks your SOL in the ChessHussle escrow program PDA — on-chain.

03

Opponent Matches & Game Starts

Your opponent signs a join transaction matching your exact wager. Both locked. Game begins.

04

Checkmate → Auto Payout

Our arbiter oracle calls declare_winner on-chain. Winner gets the full pot minus 2%. SOL hits your wallet in seconds.

🔒

Anchor Escrow Program

SOL locked in a PDA derived from game ID + host key. No one can move it except the program logic.

⚡

Solana Speed

400ms block times. Payouts land in your wallet within seconds of the game ending.

🤖

AI Anti-Cheat

Engine detection runs server-side. Cheaters are flagged; the arbiter refunds the opponent on-chain.

📊

ELO Matchmaking

Match quality score on every open game. Best matches shown first.

🛡

Non-Custodial

Funds only ever live in the smart contract PDA. We cannot touch them.
